Website Evaluations

Yale School of Art

A collaborative webpage that all students, faculty, staff, and alumns of the Yale School of Art can freely edit

  • Contrast - The colors seem randomly picked. 2 / 10.
  • Repitition - The background image repeats itself multiple times. There is no coheasion. 3 / 10.
  • Alignment - Nothing is aligned. 0 / 10.
  • Proximity - Multiple things overlap. The page's space is barely used. 0 / 10.

Crap is Good

A fairly unique art blog, focusing primarly on "contemporary visual culture." Finding the beauty of in garbage essentially.

  • Contrast - The color schemes flow together smoothly. There are no jarring colors. The colors are a little bland, but this allows the art to stick out as the main focus. 7/10.
  • There is a comforting repitition in the page's layout, but not so overly used that it is nauseating. 8 / 10.
  • Alignment - Everything is aligned perfectly. 10/10
  • Proximity - There is enough space so that things do not feel claustrophobic, while mainting a nice level of content on the page so that it does not feel barren. 9/10.
