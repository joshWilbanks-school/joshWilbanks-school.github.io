  • Cutting Edge Kade

  • Project Overview

    • Purpose: This website is being made as a buisness page for my client, Kade. Kade is a hairstylest who wants a website to show off her work, tell people about herself, and to use for marketing. This website will also contain information about where she is currently working at, what services she is currently offering, how to contact her, as well as links to her other social media.

    • Intended Users:

      1. People who are interested in Kade as a stylist
      2. People who are looking for a service to be done
      3. People who are potentially looking at hiring Kade at a new salon
      4. People who want to contact Kade
      5. People who simply love the craft and wish to see Kade's gallery
    • Overview:

      The content of the website will consist of:

      1. A home page with a brief overview of everything
      2. A gallery of her previous work
      3. A section about herself
      4. A section about the services she offers
      5. How to contact her
      6. How to find her current salon
      7. Some reviews of her

  • Page Design

      • Name: Home
      • Purpose: Brief overview of the website / introductory page
      • User(s): Customers
      • Content: A brief synopsis of the site's content with many illustrated graphics.
      • Links: There will be links in the header navbar for navigation
      • Functionality: There will be animations for transitions when scrolling
      • Name: Gallery
      • Purpose: Gallery of all of Kade's past work
      • User(s): Customers
      • Content: Many photo's of Kade's past work
      • Links: There will be links in the header navbar for navigation
      • Functionality: The gallery will automatically resize and scroll correctly
      • Name: Services
      • Purpose: To show what services Kade is available of offering
      • User(s): Customers
      • Content: A list of all of Kade's current services and their pricing, then another list of all her services she's working on and their pricing.
      • Links: There will be links in the header navbar for navigation
      • Functionality: There will be no additional functionality
      • Name: Reviews
      • Purpose: To show some of Kade's clients reviews
      • User(s): Customers
      • Content: Reviews from some of Kade's previous clients. Note: could add ability to leave reviews if site wasn't read only
      • Links: There will be links in the header navbar for navigation
      • Functionality: The review page MIGHT load reviews through http calls.
      • Name: About
      • Purpose: Give information about Kade
      • User(s): Customer
      • Content: A headshot of Kade and a biography written by her
      • Links: There will be links in the header navbar for navigation
      • Functionality: There will be no additional functionality
      • Name: Contact
      • Purpose: Give a point for users to reach out to Kade and figure out how to find her
      • User(s): Customer
      • Content: A forum for contact, her current salon information, and an interactive map of where her salon is
      • Links: There will be links in the header navbar for navigation. A link to her salon as well.
      • Functionality: Dynamic map and forum with textfields / a submit button. Using JS to connect to google forms to automatically connect with Kade.

  • Dynamic Functionality

    On the contact page there will be a map showing her current salon and a form to allow for users to contact Kade, most likely using google forms. Hopefully it will be similar in affect to this.

    Additionally, there MIGHT be functionality for users to leave reviews. Linking in with google forms would be a good way to do this. Because google forms is free and has an API, automatically updating user reviews could be done without the need for a backend.
